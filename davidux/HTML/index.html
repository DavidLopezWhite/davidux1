About Me

A common thread in my life and work has been understanding and helping people

Designer and Educator


I've worked as a freelance and contract designer with clients including Ecommerce, EdTech, Healthcare, Finance, Nonprofit, Agency, and HVAC.

I've also designed and built websites from soup to nuts for a range of clients. In these projects, I've worn many hats, and have executed web and software projects from ideation to execution and delivery. It's been a privilege to do this work, and I look forward to my next opportunity, perhaps working with your team!

Prior to that, I worked in a fast-paced digital media college as an instructor and lecturer. During this time, I taught audio editing, sound design, video production, and multimedia assembly. I still teach private lessons and perform locally from time to time.

My commitment to excellence in my design career means bringing value to others, because I am genuinely curious to know how best I can be of assistance.
